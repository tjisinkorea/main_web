I've been considering diving deeper into a specific field and getting a certification, but I haven't decided which path to go. Three options are:

One of these certs is what will consume most of my time. However I am always looking up random things. I just recently learned of Netlify, and how I can host websites for free by linking with github. So these two websites are 100% free as I have both the hosting and DNS taken care of.

It also takes less than 1 week to ship. I think it will arrive in the next few days so I will be tinkering with that! I have a bunch of micro SD cards ready to try different projects.
